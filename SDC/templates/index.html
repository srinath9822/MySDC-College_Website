Overview

      Welcome to the SDC institution. Though a relatively young university, founded in 2008 with only two courses i.e. , B.Com and BCA .The college started with only 100 stududents,now SDC has grown with many ug courses i.e, BBA , BSc and PG courses i.e, M.Com and MSc(Mathematics) with over 2500 students also with many achivements.
To Provide Affordable, quality education to a wide cross-section of society without any discrimination of caste or creed with emphasis on social justice and innovative practice in teaching using latest technologies. 

      A proud Golden Age institution, the SDC has a history as rich as it is remarkable. Since we are into competition, we have broken records and pioneered new education standards, rivaling and outpacing even our oldest peer institutions. We have rich traditions – traditions of access and opportunity for students, of academic excellence, of groundbreaking efforts, of serving our communities.. 

      At SDC, we believe every student will succeed if given the opportunity. All SDC students, regardless of their backgrounds or circumstances, succeed at the same rate. We invite you to explore more about our institution and discover how you can prosper at the fastest-rising SDC institution.At SDC, excellence and opportunity converge. 

      Smt. Danamma Channabasavaiah College of Arts, Commerce, Science and Management studies is located on NH-75, Kolar-Mulbagal by-pass road and the college is located just two kilometres away from Kolar KSRTC Bus Stand.
