Courses

      SDC offers you a wide range of courses to build your future.

B.C.A(Bachelor of Computer Application)

ELIGIBILITY
Candidates should have passed in the qualifying examinations (Pre University or Intermediate) from any recognized Secondary Education Board. Any student who has passed Diploma in Engineering (of three year duration of Government of Karnataka) with a minimum of 35% of aggregate marks in every semester / year.
DURATION
3 years split into 6 semesters
LEARNING OUTCOMES
Enables the students to identify and sharpen their IT/ programming skills.

M.Sc(Master of Science)

Candidate should have passed in B.Sc or BCA with Mathematics as Compulsory subject from any UGC recognized university with at least 50 percent aggregate.
DURATION
2 years split into 4 semesters
LEARNING OUTCOMES
Understanding of the fundamental axioms in mathematics and capability of developing ideas based on them. Inculcate mathematical reasoning. To develop ones own learning capacity. Prepare and motivate students for research studies in mathematics and related fields.

M.Com(Master of Commerce)

ELIGIBLITY
Candidate should have passed in B.Com or B.BA from any UGC recognized university with at least 50 percent aggregate (SC /ST 45 percent).
DURATION
2 years split into 4 semesters
LEARNING OUTCOMES
The entire course is outcome-based and faculty are sensitive to ensure that the outcomes for each course is defined well at the beginning of the program and shared with each student through course handouts. This ensures expectation setting is taken care of between the learner and the teacher. Interactive lectures, pedagogy that is evolving, assignments, and assessments ensure that all students achieve the specified outcomes.
